Esquites also known as vasito de elotes (little corn-cup) is a Mexican snack or antojito. It is served hot in a small or large cup and topped with varying combinations of lime juice, chile powder or hot sauce, grated cheese, and mayonnaise.

A tamale is a traditional Mesoamerican and South American dish, made of masa or dough, which is steamed in a corn husk or banana leaf.
